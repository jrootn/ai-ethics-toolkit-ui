# AI Ethics Toolkit UI

An interactive, user-centric toolkit designed to help developers, designers, and stakeholders navigate the complexities of AI ethics. This project provides a framework for building responsible, transparent, and fair AI systems through a modern UI.

As AI becomes more integrated into daily life, the ethical implications of its design and deployment are critical. This toolkit serves as a guide to:
* **Bias Mitigation:** Identifying and reducing algorithmic bias.
* **Transparency:** Making AI decision-making processes understandable.
* **Accountability:** Establishing clear guidelines for AI outcomes.
* **User Empowerment:** Ensuring users have control over their data and AI interactions.

The UI is built with a focus on accessibility and clarity, ensuring that complex ethical concepts are easy to digest and implement.
